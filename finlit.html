FinLit

Gamified Financial Literacy

Made during my time as a 2023 Kode With Klossy Scholar, FinLit is an app that seeks to facilitate the easy access to financial resources and knowledge through interactive games.

Client

Kode With Klossy

Services

Python UI & UX Design

Industries

Finance

Date

Data shows that when categorized by race or ethnic group, financial literacy rates are not evenly dispersed. The lack of financial education is a factor that significantly contributes to socioeconomic disparities. To combat this issue, FinLit seeks to facilitate the easy access to financial resources and knowledge through interactive games.
